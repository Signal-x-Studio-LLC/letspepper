## Event 1: Bell Pepper Open

**Event Name:** Bell Pepper Open — Let's Pepper Summer Series

**Date & Time:**
- Saturday, June 6, 2026
- 8:30 AM – 6:00 PM (CDT)

**Location:**
- Nature Meadows Park
- 1861 Westridge Pl, Aurora, IL 60504

**Category:** Sports

**Event Photo:** Use `social/exports/phase-1-launch/1-bell-pepper-open.png`

**Description:**

The Bell Pepper Open is the season opener of the Let's Pepper Summer Series — an underground grass volleyball tournament built for competitors.

WHAT YOU NEED TO KNOW
- Format: Grass Triples (3v3)
- Entry Fee: $120 per team
- One Division — high-level competition only
- Check-in: 8:30 AM / First serve: 9:00 AM sharp
- All teams make playoffs

FORMAT
- Pool of 4: 2 sets to 21 (cap 23)
- Pool of 3: 3 sets to 21 (cap 23)
- Playoffs: 1 game to 30 until Round 5
- Semifinals & Finals: Best of 3 (21, 21, 15)
- Switch sides every 7 points

RULES
- Call your own and be honest
- Doubles allowed unless egregious
- Hand setting serves and free balls allowed
- No open hand tipping
- Hand sets over the net must be square to your shoulders
- Let serves are NOT allowed

CASH PRIZES (scale with team count)
- 12 teams: $600 / $240
- 16 teams: $900 / $300
- 20 teams: $1,200 / $400
- 24 teams: $1,500 / $500
- 28 teams: $1,800 / $600
More teams = more money.

PARKING
- Parking lot and street parking available

GOOD TO KNOW
- Self-officiated (trust-based)
- Payment required 3 days in advance
- No refund within 24 hours of event
- Bathrooms are not available on-site (Walgreens is 3 min away)
- Full media coverage by Flickday Media

DM @letspepper.open on Instagram to register your team.

Website: https://letspepper.vercel.app/flavors/bell-pepper-open
